DC exit, cloud migration (J2C) and modernization

Insurance & Financial Services

Europe's first successful modernization and migration of Guidewire to cloud for a UK-based Automobile and Home Insurer serving 500,000 quotes a day, 4 million customers.

Challenge

As business was growing along with data center cost & complexity, the client wanted to transition to cloud-first, improve its market share and deliver better, faster customer journeys.

Solution

  • DC exit and migration of Guidewire, other apps (100+), data, and infra to Azure
  • Modernization of legacy applications and infrastructure
  • Implementation of cloud-native architecture
  • APIfication and automation of processes
  • Data modernization and migration

Results

  • Increased performance by 1.6 times
  • 4 to 5 times faster insurance adjustments
  • Legacy-free IT estate
  • APIfication and Automation
  • Data modernization
  • Operational cost savings

Technologies Used

Azure, Azure VMware Solution (AVS), Snowflake, Guidewire

Legacy modernization and cloud migration

Healthcare & Financial Services

Modernization and cloud migration of legacy and complex apps and databases for an APAC based health insurance and financial services major.

Challenge

Client had complex legacy applications that were difficult to maintain, had high time to market, and used outdated tech. The UX was old, and the apps did not scale. They needed to modernize their IT landscape to stay competitive in the market and respond to growing user needs.

Solution

  • Modernization and cloud migration of legacy and complex apps and databases to Azure
  • Refactoring of 20+ applications with 2 million+ lines of code
  • Technology stack modernization (Java, .NET, Sybase, SQL Server, DB2, WebSphere MQ)
  • Implementation of cloud-native architecture
  • Performance optimization and scalability improvements

Results

  • Improved performance (2.5x) and scalability
  • Tech debt reduction by 70%
  • Reduced change lead time
  • Higher deployment frequency

Technologies Used

Azure, Java, .NET, Sybase, SQL Server, DB2, WebSphere MQ
