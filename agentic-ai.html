Our technology experience

AWS Bedrock, Bedrock AgentCore, Strands Agents and Nova Models

Leverage Amazon Bedrock to build and scale generative AI applications with access to leading foundation models from Amazon, Anthropic, AI21 Labs, Cohere, Meta, Stability AI, and more. Our expertise includes model selection, RAG implementation, fine-tuning, and deployment of production-ready Gen AI solutions on AWS infrastructure.

Microsoft Foundry/ AI Foundry

Implement comprehensive Gen AI solutions using Microsoft AI Foundry, including Azure OpenAI Service, Azure AI Studio, and Copilot frameworks. We specialize in building enterprise-grade AI applications, custom copilots, and integrated AI experiences that seamlessly work with Microsoft 365 and Azure ecosystems.

Google Vertex AI

Build and deploy machine learning models and Gen AI applications on Google Cloud Platform. Our experience spans Vertex AI Model Garden, custom model training, AutoML capabilities, and integration with Google's Gemini models for enterprise AI solutions with advanced MLOps and governance.

Langchain and LangGraph

Develop sophisticated agentic AI applications using LangChain and LangGraph frameworks for orchestration, tool integration, and multi-agent systems. We create intelligent workflows, RAG pipelines, and autonomous agents that can reason, plan, and execute complex business processes with human-like capabilities.

OpenAI

Integrate OpenAI's GPT models, embeddings, and fine-tuning APIs to create intelligent applications. Our expertise includes prompt engineering, function calling, assistant APIs, and building custom solutions using OpenAI's advanced language models for various enterprise use cases including content generation, analysis, and automation.

Hugging Face

Utilize Hugging Face's model hub and transformers library to access thousands of pre-trained models, fine-tune custom solutions, and deploy production-ready AI applications. Our experience includes model selection, fine-tuning, evaluation, and deployment across cloud and on-premises environments.
