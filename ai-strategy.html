Our perspective & approach

Our perspective

VisionTomoro has been formed as a next gen, premier, neo-consulting and digital service provider. We blend consulting and deep engineering. VisionTomoro strongly believes that having a strong AI strategy is key for enterprise wide AI adoption and getting the business value (ROI) from AI investments. A strong AI strategy should focus on business, people, responsible usage of AI and technology. Successful AI strategy should embed data, infrastructure and security.

Our approach

  • Start with setting up a strong AI strategy involving business, IT, product/ engineering and functional and leadership teams
  • Create a clear business case for AI investments detailing the expected benefits, costs, ROI, and risks
  • Identify and prioritize high-impact use cases for AI investments
  • Setup a AI Center of Excellence (CoE) to drive AI adoption and governance
  • Conduct an assessment for AI readiness across strategy, business value, AI, data, technology, people and operations pillars
  • Setup Responsible AI principles and governance framework
  • Ensure that evals, guardrails, observability and monitoring are in place
  • Identify KPIs and metrics to measure the success of AI investments
  • Perform a POC, and based on the results move it to production.
  • Communicate the results to the business and stakeholders
