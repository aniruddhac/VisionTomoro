Application Portfolio Rationalization - Optimize Your IT Investments

76% of CIOs says application sprawl is a huge challenge and are planning for app rationalization. 78% of CIOs and CFOs will be forced to accelerate or enact formal technical debt management practices by 2025, due to project delays or failures caused by unresolved technical debt.

Benefits and challenges

Benefits

  • Reduced IT complexity and operational costs
  • Improved alignment between IT and business goals
  • Optimized application portfolio for better performance
  • Enhanced agility and faster time-to-market
  • Reduced technical debt and maintenance burden
  • Better risk management and compliance
  • Clear roadmap for modernization and cloud migration

Challenges

  • Complex application dependencies and interconnections
  • Lack of comprehensive application inventory
  • Business resistance to change
  • Difficulty in measuring application business value
  • Legacy applications with outdated technology
  • Insufficient documentation and knowledge
  • Cost and resource constraints for rationalization
